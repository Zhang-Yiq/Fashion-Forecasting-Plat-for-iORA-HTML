Security Best Practices

Protecting your data is our top priority. Follow these tips to keep your iORA account safe.

Strong Passwords

Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using common words or personal info.

Two-Factor Auth

Enable 2FA for an extra layer of security when logging in from new devices.

Phishing Alert

iORA will never ask for your password via email. Always check the sender's address.

Regular Updates

Keep your browser and mobile app updated to benefit from the latest security patches.
