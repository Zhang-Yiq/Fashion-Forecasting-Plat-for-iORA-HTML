Our Journey

Twenty-five years of sartorial excellence and growth.

1997

Founding

iORA Fashion Pte Ltd established in Singapore on May 8th, laying the groundwork for a regional fashion powerhouse.

1998

Brand Launch

Official launch of the iORA brand format, introducing our first collection of ready-to-wear essentials.

2005

Regional Expansion

Strategic expansion into Southeast Asian markets, bringing our climate-adapted designs to a broader audience.

2015

Product Diversification

Significant expansion of product lines to include accessories and specialized outerwear for the urban traveler.

2023

AI Integration

Pioneering the use of AI sales forecasting and data-driven design to optimize seasonal inventory and sustainability.
